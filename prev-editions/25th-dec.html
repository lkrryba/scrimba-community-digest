Wrap up 🐈‍⬛

And so to wish you fantastic holidays, we're joined by Ivca's lovely code helper Megg.

This festive period always brings very fond childhood memories of Ukrainian winter joy, comfort food and folk songs. Mainly "Shchedryk" or as it's better known in the Anglophone world, Carol of the Bells (aka Home Alone soundtrack 🤣). Everyone I met in the UK always got surprised that such a Christmas melody is a really popular folk song that I grew up with and always thought it was cute being the musical theme of Home Alone.

The song was written in 1916 by Ukrainian composer Mykola Leontovich and titled "Shchedryk". The song tells the tale of a swallow flying into a household to proclaim the plentiful year that the family will have. The song's title is derived from the Ukrainian word "shchedryj" which means "bountiful", "generous", "munificent".
