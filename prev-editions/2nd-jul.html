New Hires 📣

Please join me in celebrating 2 more of our members getting jobs! 👏 🥳

Micha

Micha has been hired as an apprentice software engineer with a state agency! They finished the Career Path at the end of March and immediately started applying for jobs.

Micha didn't they didn't hear anything for a month after the apprentice interview 😬 but it was worth the wait as, after a quick reference check, they came back with an offer! 🎊

Waldo

Waldo has landed their first job as a frontend dev, after 1.5 years of study and practice!

Waldo says Scrimba helped a ton when getting into React, so he gives special thanks to Bob for his clear and concise lessons! 💜

JavaScript was the biggest hurdle for Waldo to overcome. They say once you beat that, other tech will make more sense. We think that's sound advice….really worth nailing those JS fundamentals.

Ask an Expert 💻

This week, Leanne was joined on the Ask an Expert live stream by Scrimba tutor and bootcamp leader Guil Hernandez, who shared a tonne of knowledge about learning how to learn!

Scrimba Podcast

Meet Ollie Church 🇬🇧!

Ollie started out as an actor. He took up coding as a hobby during a lockdown and made a puzzle game inspired by an escape room where he worked at the time. He now works in fintech!

Ollie joined this week's podcast to talk about motivation, career change, goal-setting, choosing portfolio projects, and dealing with rejection - something that actors are maybe even more familiar with than new developers!
