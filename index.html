Gaby has a 3-month internship that might lead to a junior frontend position. She started coding in June 2021.

Congrats to Honey G on landing a job for a company in Singapore!

Podcast 🎙️

Meet Wemerson Queiroz 🇧🇷! Wemerson never thought he was going to become a developer. He started studying computer science, dropped out, and spent ten years working in sales because he wanted to earn money immediately.

Realizing he was unhappy (after all, money is not everything), he learned to code and got his first developer job in no time!

In this podcast episode, you'll learn how far HTML, CSS, and Javascript can get you and how important it is to follow your passion. You'll also hear about Wemerson's unusual interview process and why taking a chance sometimes pays off.
